About AS Exports

Leading Manufacturer of Poly Packaging Solutions

AS Exports is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of high-quality poly packaging products based in Surat, Gujarat, India. With modern manufacturing facilities and a strong focus on product quality, we provide reliable packaging solutions to industries across domestic and international markets.

Our product range includes PP/HDPE woven sacks, BOPP laminated bags, FIBC jumbo bags, sling bags, tarpaulins, dunnage bags, leno mesh bags, and woven fabric products. These packaging solutions are widely used in agriculture, food grains, chemicals, fertilizers, construction, and logistics industries.

Through advanced technology, skilled manpower, and strict quality control processes, AS Exports ensures durable, cost-effective, and export-grade packaging products for customers worldwide.

Our Manufacturing Process

Our manufacturing process ensures consistent quality and durability in every poly packaging product we produce.

From raw material to finished packaging products with strict quality control

Raw Material

High-quality PP / HDPE granules are selected for durable packaging products.

Extrusion

Granules are melted and extruded into strong plastic tapes.

Weaving

Plastic tapes are woven into strong fabric using circular looms.

Lamination

Lamination improves strength, durability, and moisture resistance.

Printing

Advanced printing machines apply branding and product details.

Cutting & Stitching

Fabric is cut and stitched into bags, sacks, or jumbo bags.

Quality Inspection

Each product undergoes strict quality testing before dispatch.

Packaging & Export

Products are packed securely and shipped to global clients.
