Durable & Custom Tarps for Everyday Applications

Tarpaulins are versatile protective coverings widely used across industries, agriculture, construction, and transportation. Designed to shield goods, equipment, and materials from weather, dust, and damage, these tarps are crafted from high-quality materials such as heavy-duty polyethylene, HDPE, and laminated fabrics. With reinforced edges, rust-resistant grommets, and customizable sizes, tarpaulins offer exceptional durability, water resistance, and reliable protection for everyday and industrial applications.

Why Choose Tarp from AS Exports?

A tarp is a large sheet of strong, flexible, water-resistant cloth. Tarps often have reinforced eyelets at the corners and along the sides to form attachment points for rope, allowing them to be tied down or suspended. Modern tarps are made from heat-sealed woven polyethylene (HDPE).

There are various types of tarps available in different materials and thicknesses for various applications such as Plastic Tarp, Waterproof Tarp, Canvas Tarp, Heavy-duty Tarp, Roof Tarp, Truck Tarp, and Custom Tarp.
