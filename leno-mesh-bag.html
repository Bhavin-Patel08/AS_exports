Leno Mesh Bags for Durable and Breathable Packaging

Leno Mesh Bags are widely used in agricultural packaging for onions, potatoes, vegetables, and fruits.

What Are Leno/Mesh Bags?

Woven bags, or Leno bags, are ideal as they are solid, form-stable, and breathable. While we make Leno mesh bags for packaging seasonal products, other products like firewood require the production of Leno mesh bags year-round. Mesh Bags are made of the finest polypropylene materials, known for their durability and flexibility. This choice of material ensures that our bags are not only strong but also lightweight, making them perfect for a variety of uses, from agricultural to industrial applications.

Applications Of Mesh Bags in Agriculture & Industry

  • Our Mesh Bags (also known as Vegetable Bags) are ideal for packaging agricultural products such as fruits and vegetables, providing proper ventilation to maintain freshness.
  • The mesh material is produced on both flat Sulzer and circular looms, ensuring high-quality and durable manufacturing standards.
  • In the seafood industry, Leno mesh bags are widely used for packaging and transporting shellfish, allowing adequate airflow to keep the products fresh.
  • These bags come equipped with drawstrings and labels for effective branding and accurate weight markings.
  • Our mesh fruit bags are manufactured using premium quality materials in accordance with industry standards.
  • Available in various sizes and customizations to meet client requirements, these durable and easy-to-carry bags are also widely used for shopping purposes.
  • Mesh onion bags are ideal for packaging onions and many other commodities such as peanuts, cabbage, citrus fruits, shellfish, firewood, and other miscellaneous products.
  • They are also suitable for different storage needs such as athletic equipment and laundry, with a drawstring tape for easy closure.
  • Used in retail and logistics for easy handling, these bags are ideal for markets and supermarkets, while their attractive and colorful designs enhance product presentation.

Key Features of Our Mesh Bags

  • Our Mesh bags provide a secure and clean solution for packing agricultural products, consumables, and perishables.
  • Their lightweight and durable design, along with moisture resistance, makes them a perfect choice for safe storage and transportation.
  • These bags offer excellent breathability, preventing odor transfer to packed fruits and vegetables due to their ventilated mesh structure.
  • The mesh structure also helps prevent fungal growth on both the bag surface and the packed contents.
  • They act as a preventive measure against sweating and rotting of agricultural produce, ensuring safe storage and transportation of fruits, vegetables, and other food products.
  • With minimal wastage and spillage, these bags contribute to efficient packing and handling processes.
  • Polypropylene mesh is well suited for securing products during transportation while offering excellent breathability and product visibility.
  • Polypropylene mesh bags are also environmentally friendly and provide an economical packaging solution.
