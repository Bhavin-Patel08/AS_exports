Dunnage bags for Reliable Cargo Protection

Dunnage Bags are advanced cargo securing solutions widely used in the transportation and logistics industry. These inflatable bags are placed between cargo inside containers, trucks, or railcars to prevent movement during transit. Made from high-strength materials such as polypropylene woven fabric and polyethylene liners, Dunnage Bags provide excellent stability, shock absorption, and reliable protection for goods during shipping and storage.

What are Dunnage Bags?

Dunnage bags safeguard cargo during transportation. The air-filled bags are used to restrain movement of cargo loads in trucks, containers, or railcars. They fill voids, brace loads, absorb vibrations, and protect cargo from in-transit damage. These reusable bags come in various sizes to fit different cargo dimensions. They are widely used in the shipping industry to secure cargo within containers, trailers, or railcars, ensuring safe and damage-free deliveries. Dunnage airbags are inflated with air and placed between items in a shipping container or truck. They prevent large items from shifting during transit, protecting against damage to the shipment. Dunnage Bags are specifically designed to secure and stabilize cargo during transport. They are moisture and pressure resistant, making them a durable and reliable packaging solution.

Applications Of Dunnage Airbags in Shipping & Transport

  • Our dunnage bags are the ideal solution for protecting valuable cargo during transportation.
  • In order to secure cargo in shipping containers, trucks, and railcars and avoid movement or potential damage during transit, these are in high demand in the shipping and logistics industries.
  • Compressed air is used to inflate the polymer bag; this air is kept inside by an air-lock mechanism. This assures that during transit, the dunnage airbags maintain the appropriate pressure.
  • The dunnage bag, once fastened between shipping containers, keeps the cargo from moving laterally or longitudinally and stabilizes it there for the duration of the trip.
  • Cargo bags aid in stabilizing oddly shaped objects that are difficult to fasten with conventional techniques, guaranteeing their position throughout transit.
  • To keep the appropriate distance between bulk items, these shipping airbags are utilized.
