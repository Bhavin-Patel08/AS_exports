Tarpaulin Fabric for Industrial & Commercial Use

Tarpaulin fabric is a high-performance material widely used in industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications. Made from durable polymers like polyethylene and reinforced woven fabrics, it provides excellent resistance to water, UV rays, and tearing. Ideal for covering goods, machinery, and construction sites, this fabric ensures reliable protection and longevity. With customizable thicknesses, coatings, and sizes, tarpaulin fabric delivers unmatched strength, flexibility, and safety for demanding environments.

Why Choose Tarpaulin Fabric from AS Exports?

Tarpaulin fabric is a woven high-density polyethylene fabric with a laminated coating. It is a medium-duty fabric that is durable, lightweight, tear-resistant, and impermeable. The weatherproof design and UV stabilization ensure long-lasting protection against environmental factors.

Tarpaulin material is available in roll form. With precision engineering, AS Exports’ tarpaulin fabric is designed to withstand sun, rain, wind, and other environmental conditions. Reinforced edges and aluminum eyelets provide secure fastening, making them suitable for demanding agricultural, commercial, and industrial applications.

Tarpaulin fabric is incredibly versatile and finds applications across many industries. AS Exports' tarpaulin material ensures superior performance and longevity, offering excellent durability, design, and functionality.

  • Tarpaulin sheets are widely used in agriculture, transportation, water storage, packaging, and various made-up products.
