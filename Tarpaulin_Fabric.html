Tarpaulin Fabric for Industrial & Commercial Use

Why Choose Tarpaulin Fabric from AS Exports?

Tarpaulin fabric is a woven high-density polyethylene fabric with a laminated coating. It is a medium-duty fabric that is durable, lightweight, tear-resistant, and impermeable. The weatherproof design and UV stabilization ensure long-lasting protection against environmental factors.

Tarpaulin material is available in roll form. With precision engineering, AS Exports’ tarpaulin fabric is designed to withstand sun, rain, wind, and other environmental conditions. Reinforced edges and aluminum eyelets provide secure fastening, making them suitable for demanding agricultural, commercial, and industrial applications.

Tarpaulin fabric is incredibly versatile and finds applications across many industries. AS Exports' tarpaulin material ensures superior performance and longevity, offering excellent durability, design, and functionality.

Applications Of Tarpaulin Fabric

  • Our tarpaulin fabric is designed to provide superior protection and durability. As leading exporters of heavy-duty tarpaulin fabric, we ensure the highest standards of quality and reliability.
  • Available in various sizes and colors as per client requirements at affordable prices.
  • Tarpaulin sheets are widely used in agriculture, transportation, water storage, packaging, and various made-up products.
  • Used to cover trucks, aircraft, tempos, trailer trucks, railway wagons, industrial machines, and agricultural products to protect them from harsh weather and UV rays.
  • Tarpaulin material is used for bale wrapping, coverings, carpet backing, tufting, nets, curtains, handbags, and wall coverings.
  • Used at construction sites to cover equipment, vehicles, and materials, protecting them from rain, UV rays, and other weather elements.
  • Farmers use tarpaulin fabrics for livestock shelters, hay storage, equipment protection, and temporary grain storage.
  • Tarpaulins are used in sporting events like baseball and softball to cover fields during rain delays.
  • Used for advertising applications such as billboards and banners due to their durability in outdoor environments.
  • Our tarpaulin fabric is available in roll form and can be customized according to your specifications.
