PP FABRIC/HDPE FABRIC Rolls for industrial Applications

PP/HDPE Woven Fabric Sheets are designed for versatility and durability in industrial and commercial applications. Made from high-quality polypropylene (PP) or high-density polyethylene (HDPE), these sheets provide excellent resistance to moisture, tearing, and UV exposure. Available in customizable sizes, thicknesses, and colors, they are ideal for packaging, covering goods, agricultural applications, and protective uses. The robust woven structure ensures long-term reliability, while optional BOPP lamination allows for enhanced printing and branding.

Why Choose HDPE Sheets?

Our woven PP or HDPE Sheets are appreciated for features like high bearing strength, moisture resistance, and low density. They are made using high-quality PP (Polypropylene) or HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) plastic granules. The material used to make the fabric is PP/HDPE granules. With granules, the tapes are made and these tapes after getting weaved to form the fabric. This fabric is offered by us in standard as well as customized lengths, weights, gussets, meshes, and colors to suit the demands of our clients. We ensure that our customers get top-quality PP or HDPE sheets. Since this process is based on calculations and material ratio, we make sure that no quality issue arises during the process.

Woven sheets are available in both laminated & unlaminated forms. Due to its low weight its best suited for Wrapping and covering for diverse industries across the world.

PP/HDPE Sheets for Industrial Applications

  • PP woven sheets are used for wrapping, covering, and protecting items during storage and transportation.
  • These sheets are often used in agriculture, logistics, and construction sectors for covering and securing various items.
  • In the construction industry, they are used for scaffolding, temporary enclosures, and protective covers.
  • They are used as ground covers to suppress weed growth, conserve soil moisture, and regulate temperature in agriculture.
  • We offer HDPE / PP woven fabric in flat & circular form, used in chemical & fertilizer companies for wrapping products, etc.
  • PP / HDPE sheets are used for wrapping paper rolls, textile fabrics, old cloth, all types of rubber tyres, steel coils, galvanized pipes, etc.
  • These sheets have a low coefficient of friction, long-term durability, and high impact strength.
